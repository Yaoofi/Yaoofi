Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 11/11/2024

1. What Data We Collect

Our app may collect and process the following types of user information:

  • User Messages: Our app receives and processes messages sent by users on the Discord platform. These messages are processed temporarily to respond within the chat context but are not stored beyond the operational needs of the app.
  • Usernames and Mentions: We collect and process usernames and user tags (mentions) within messages to personalize responses.

Note: We do not collect sensitive personal data or store messages long-term.

2. How We Use Data

User data is used solely for:

  • Responding to user messages based on pre-programmed responses or AI-generated replies via Google Generative AI.
  • Creating personalized responses based on recognized keywords and phrases in messages.

3. Use of Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Generative AI: To generate AI-based responses, we use Google Generative AI (Gemini). Google’s Privacy Policy applies to interactions with this service.
  • Discord API: We use the Discord API to process messages and bot interactions. Discord’s Privacy Policy applies to all users interacting with the bot on this platform.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share users’ personal information with other entities. Data sent to Google Generative AI is limited to the contents of messages and is used solely to generate responses.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to ensure the security of the data we process. We implement standard procedures to secure data during transmission and processing. However, please note that no online platform can guarantee complete data security.

6. User Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), users have the following rights:

  • Right to Access and Rectification: Users can access or rectify certain information by interacting with the bot or via the Discord platform.
  • Right to Erasure: Since we do not store user data, requests for data erasure do not apply to our app.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may periodically update this Privacy Policy. Users will be notified of any material changes within the bot’s message feed or Discord channel.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Discord server.
